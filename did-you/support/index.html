Help with Did You?

Support

A few quick answers, without the runaround.

Did You? is published by 1970545 Alberta Inc. You can use the app without creating an account.

Where are my answers stored?

Your answers and personal check-ins are stored locally by the app. They are not synced to an account.

Can I undo an answer?

Yes. After answering or passing, use the undo control to return to the previous question.

Do I have to answer every question?

No. Answer one, answer several, pass any question, or close the app whenever the moment feels complete.

How do I add a personal check-in?

Open Just for me, then choose one of the five inspirations or write a daily check-in in your own words. Use Show me five more whenever you want a different set of ideas.

How do I change the language?

Use the language control during the first introduction, or open the more menu from the main screen.

How do I remove my local data?

Deleting the app removes its local data from the device, subject to any device backups you maintain.

Still need help?

Email dotnebulaplatform@gmail.com. Include your device model, iOS or iPadOS version, and a short description of what happened. Please do not include private answers or personal check-in text.

简体中文

帮助与支持

先从几个最常见的疑问开始。

Did You? 由 1970545 Alberta Inc. 发布。使用这个 App 不需要注册账户。

我的答案保存在哪里？

答案和个人自问由 App 保存在本机，不会同步到某个账户。

回答以后可以撤销吗？

可以。回答或跳过以后，使用撤销按钮即可返回上一句问话。

我必须回应所有问句吗？

不必。你可以只回应一个、多回应几个、跳过任何一句，或者在觉得这一刻已经足够时关闭 App。

怎样添加每日自问？

打开“问问自己”，从五条灵感中选一句，或者亲手写一句每天想问的话。想换些灵感时，可以点“换一组”。

怎样更改语言？

在首次介绍页面使用语言按钮，或者从主界面的更多菜单中更改。

怎样删除本地数据？

删除 App 会移除设备上的 App 本地数据，但仍可能受到你所保留设备备份的影响。

还需要帮助？

请发送邮件至 dotnebulaplatform@gmail.com。联系时可以注明设备型号、iOS 或 iPadOS 版本和事情经过，但请不要附上私密答案或个人自问文字。
