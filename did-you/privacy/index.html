Did You?

Privacy Policy

Effective July 21, 2026

Did You? is published by 1970545 Alberta Inc. The app is designed to give you a small, private moment in your day. We do not require an account and we do not sell personal information.

What stays on your device

Your yes/no answers, passes, personal check-in text, and app preferences are stored locally by the app. We do not intentionally transmit this content to our servers or to our analytics provider. Your operating system may include local app data in device backups according to the backup settings you control.

Privacy-friendly analytics

We use TelemetryDeck to understand how the app is used and decide what to improve. It receives an anonymized installation identifier, app and device information such as app version, operating system version and device type, and product-interaction events.

Product-interaction events may include:

  • when onboarding or an app session starts or finishes;
  • app language and session duration;
  • which built-in catalog question was shown and its category;
  • whether a question was answered or passed, and response time; and
  • aggregate counts such as questions shown, answered, or passed.

We deliberately do not send whether your answer was “yes” or “no,” the text of a personal check-in, or the content of any personal answer. TelemetryDeck states that it does not store IP addresses and does not use this information to track you across other companies’ apps or websites.

No advertising or cross-app tracking

Did You? contains no third-party advertising and does not use collected information for advertising, data brokerage, or cross-app tracking.

Retention and deletion

You can remove locally stored app data by deleting the app, subject to any device backups you maintain. Analytics retention is managed through TelemetryDeck. Because analytics identifiers are anonymized and we do not maintain user accounts, we generally cannot connect an analytics record back to a particular person.

Children

The app is not designed to collect identifying information from children. It has no account creation, social features, messaging, advertising, or user-to-user content sharing.

Changes

If our data practices change, we will update this policy and its effective date before the change applies to a released version of the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, email dotnebulaplatform@gmail.com or visit the Did You? support page.

简体中文

隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 21 日

Did You? 由 1970545 Alberta Inc. 发布。这个 App 希望每天给你留下一小段安静、私密的时间。我们不要求注册账户，也不会出售个人信息。

留在设备上的内容

你的“是／否”答案、跳过记录、个人自问文字以及 App 偏好设置由 App 保存在本机。我们不会主动把这些内容发送到自己的服务器或分析服务商。操作系统可能会按照你设置的备份方式，将本地 App 数据包含在设备备份中。

保护隐私的使用分析

我们使用 TelemetryDeck 了解 App 的使用方式，以决定应该改进什么。它会收到匿名化的安装标识、App 版本、系统版本、设备类型，以及产品交互事件。

这些事件可能包括首次介绍或使用会话的开始与结束、语言、会话时长、出现过的内置问句编号与类别、用户是回答还是跳过、响应时间，以及展示、回答和跳过的总数。

我们刻意不发送你的答案究竟是“是”还是“否”，不发送个人自问的文字，也不发送任何个人答案内容。TelemetryDeck 声明不会保存 IP 地址，也不会利用这些信息跨其他公司的 App 或网站追踪你。

没有广告或跨 App 追踪

Did You? 不含第三方广告，也不会将收集的信息用于广告、数据交易或跨 App 追踪。

保留与删除

删除 App 可以移除 App 在本机保存的数据，但仍可能受到你所保留设备备份的影响。分析数据由 TelemetryDeck 按其保留规则处理。由于分析标识经过匿名化，而且我们没有用户账户，通常无法把某条分析记录对应到某个具体的人。

儿童

本 App 不以收集儿童身份信息为目的，也没有账户、社交、聊天、广告或用户间内容分享功能。

政策变更

如果数据处理方式发生变化，我们会在新版本实施变化前更新本政策及其生效日期。

联系我们

如果你对隐私有疑问，请发送邮件至 dotnebulaplatform@gmail.com，或者访问 Did You? 支持页面。
